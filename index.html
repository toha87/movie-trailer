The Matrix

I know you're out there. I can feel you now. I know that you're afraid... you're afraid of us. You're afraid of change. I don't know the future. --Neo

I watched this movie for the first time when we were 12 years old, since then it is one of my favorite movies. I was particularly impressed with how the main actor Keanu Reese played his role. I thought when I grow up, I’ll definitely buy myself the same coat as Keanu’s.

This film describes another world in which people and machines fighting with each other, other world looks like code from which everything is made. The protagonist named Neo is the chosen one and must save our planet.
